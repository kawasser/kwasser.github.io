SHELF MOTIVATION

With a team, I designed and developed a virtual book club Android app where users could add books to their libraries, read reviews, and form clubs with friends to discuss these books. We used agile development to structure the project. I worked on both front and UI and back end interation with JSON data and the database.

SANITY

With a team, I designed and developed a budget management IOS app where users could keep track of their spending. When they made a transaction, they could apply it to a specific budget for a specific category. Their spending was kept track of and visualized. I worked on the UI, and interacte with the firebase database. We coded in Xcode and used mainly swift.

JEOPARDY

I used java in eclipse to make a desktop applicaiton using a MYSQL database to store questions and correct answers. Multiple players could compete with one another answering timed questions and batteling until final jeopardy where a winner would be declared.
